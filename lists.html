Thai Seafood Sauce

This Thai seafood sauce recipe is notoriously delicous and is found all over Thailand. It is an excellent and complimentary sauce to seafood of all sorts. I love it because it combines all elements of spicy, sour, salty, and sweet. The ingredients are used as a guide only and not as strict amounts. It is advised to add and taste to your own preference!

Instructions

  1. Add the Thai birds eye chilies and peeled garlic into a mortar and pestle. Toss in 1/2 teaspoon of salt, start pounding until chilies and garlic are smashed. Make sure that all of the oils are released and seeds are crushed. The alternative way is the throw everything into a blender but the traditional way is the mortar and pestle. 🌶 | 🧄
    • Important Side Note: Pound with caution! Do not look directly down into the mortar and pestle while pounding to avoid chili seeds flying up into your eyes! You can use a paper towel to cover the top of the mortar while pounding instead for added safety. ⚠
